About Us

Serving the Sikh community
of the East Bay since 1984

Sri Guru Singh Sabha El Sobrante was founded by a small group of Punjabi families who settled in the Richmond and El Sobrante areas in the early 1980s. What began as weekly prayers in a shared community hall has grown into a dedicated Gurdwara Sahib serving hundreds of families from across the East Bay.

Our mission is rooted in the teachings of Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji — that all people are equal in the eyes of Waheguru, and all are welcome at the Gurdwara regardless of background, faith, or circumstance. The langar (community kitchen) has served a free meal to every visitor every Sunday since our doors first opened. No one leaves hungry.

Beyond Sunday diwan and weekly kirtan, the Gurdwara operates the Khalsa School, teaching Punjabi language, Gurmukhi script, and Sikh history to children of the sangat — ensuring the next generation stays connected to their heritage.
